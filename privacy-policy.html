Privacy Policy for Aesthetic Sticky Notes

Last updated: February 2025

Aesthetic Sticky Notes ("the extension") is a Chrome extension that replaces your new tab with a sticky-notes page. This policy describes what data the extension uses and where it goes.

Data stored on your device

The extension stores the following in your browser using Chrome's storage (local and/or synced):

  • Notes: Note content, titles, tags, colors, size/position, and formatting. Used only to show and edit your notes on the new tab.
  • Settings: Your chosen theme, optional name for the greeting, optional location for weather, search engine preference, and temperature unit. Used only to personalize the new-tab page.
  • Background: The URL of the last loaded background image for fast loading on the next new tab. Used only for display.

This data stays in your browser. We do not collect, upload, or have access to it on any server.

Data sent to other services

The extension may send data to the following services only when you use the related feature:

FeatureData sentServicePurpose
WeatherLocation you entered in SettingsOpen-MeteoTo show current weather on the new-tab greeting. Only if you set a location.
Background imageNone (request for a random image)Developer's proxy, then UnsplashTo load a nature photo as the new-tab background.
Ask AIText you select in a note and your promptDeveloper's proxy (forwards to an AI provider)To generate or refine text. Only when you click "Ask AI" and enter a prompt.

The extension does not send your full notes or other stored data to these services except the text you explicitly select for "Ask AI." We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

Data we do not collect

The extension does not collect or transmit analytics, browsing history, or other tabs. No personal data is collected beyond what you optionally enter (name, location) for the greeting and weather.

Your choices and control

Notes and settings are stored only in your browser. You can remove them by clearing the extension's storage or uninstalling the extension. Do not set a location in Settings if you do not want any location sent to Open-Meteo. Do not use "Ask AI" if you do not want text sent to the AI provider. Uninstalling the extension removes its access to Chrome storage.

Changes and contact

We may update this privacy policy from time to time; the "Last updated" date will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes means you accept the updated policy. For questions, contact the developer via the link on the Chrome Web Store listing.
